The Best Movies According to Diego

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Ponyo (2008)

This is my favourite Japanese movie about water and simple things. A little fish wants to become human. The ocean is full of magic and heart.

Perfect Days (2023)

A Japanese film about a man who cleans public toilets in Tokyo. He finds beauty in water, trees, shadows and everyday routines. Very simple and peaceful.

Wolf Children (2012)

Beautiful story about a mother raising her two special children near the countryside. Rain, rivers, baths and cooking — small moments full of love.
